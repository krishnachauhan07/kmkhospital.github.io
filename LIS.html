Home / Laboratory information system(LIS)

1. Sample Tracking

LabWare allows for the efficient tracking of patient samples from the point of collection to analysis and reporting. Each sample is assigned a unique identifier that is used to monitor its progress through the laboratory.


2. Sample Barcode Scanning

Lab technicians can use barcode scanners to quickly and accurately input sample information into the system, reducing the risk of manual data entry errors.


3. Test Order Management

Physicians can electronically order laboratory tests through the hospital's Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, and these orders are seamlessly integrated into LabWare for scheduling and processing.


4. Results Reporting

LabWare generates and stores test results, making them accessible to authorized healthcare professionals through the hospital's EHR or a dedicated web portal. Results can also be automatically sent to patients if desired.


5. Quality Control

The system includes tools for monitoring and maintaining the quality of laboratory tests, ensuring that results are accurate and reliable.


6. Inventory Management

LabWare helps manage laboratory inventory, tracking reagents, consumables, and equipment, and can generate alerts when supplies are running low.

8. Compliance and Reporting

LabWare can generate compliance reports for regulatory bodies and ensure that the laboratory meets all necessary standards and requirements.


9. Security and Access Control

The system employs strict access controls and audit trails to protect patient data and maintain data integrity.


10. Integration

LabWare can integrate with other hospital systems, such as the EHR, billing systems, and patient management systems, to ensure seamless information exchange.
